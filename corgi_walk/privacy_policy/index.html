Privacy Policy for Corgi Walk Application

Effective Date: 15 Apr 2025

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. Corgi Walk application ("we", "our", "us") is designed to function without collecting any personally identifiable information. We are committed to providing a secure experience while ensuring that any data used is solely to enhance your gaming experience.

2. Data Collection

No Personal Information Collected: We do not collect or store any personal details such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Local Game Progress Data: Our app stores information about your game progress (for example, the number of stars earned per level) directly on your mobile device. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

3. Data Usage

Local Storage: The game progress data stored locally is used only to improve your gaming experience, such as keeping track of your achievements and unlocking levels.

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share, sell, or rent your data to any third parties.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our app does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies.

5. Data Security

Although we do not collect personal data, we implement reasonable security measures to protect the integrity of the data stored on your device.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically.
